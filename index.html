FAQ

How much does it cost to add a user/card?

Every plan includes a certain number of users and corporate cards:

  • Advanced – 2 cards/users
  • Premium – 5 cards/users

On the Premium plan you can also order additional cards for €3/mo and add additional users for €2/mo.

What is the spending limit per card?

You can set the custom spending limit for each corporate card in your company. The maximum spending limit per Penta’s corporate card is 50.000€ per month – 25.000€ in online spent and 25.000€ in offline spent, including cash withdrawals. Each additional card will have an additional spending limit, therefore the overall company limit increases.

Can I download my transactions to give to my accountant?

You can download your transactions, for example as a MT940 file with the attached receipts and send these to your tax accountant. But with Penta you can also give your accountant a dedicated access to your account.
